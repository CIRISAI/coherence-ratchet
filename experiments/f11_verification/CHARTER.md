# F-11 verification — the adversarial "purely positive" pass — charter

**Date:** 2026-05-22. F-11 (the joint multi-rung backward P_ω is a documented
no-go) is a huge claim — it re-grounds the universal-scale tier and retracts
lake axioms. Before it is written into the v2 manuscript it gets the same
adversarial scrutiny a dramatic positive result would get. This is that pass.

## What F-11 fired on

- An exhaustive construction tree: hard projector; soft additive ansatz;
  R1 (non-additive) / R2 (history-space) / R3 (derived cross-rung); the R1∧R2
  conjunction; fractal/RG-nested; holographic/MERA-AdS; holonomic.
- **Two theorems.** T1 (holographic): a holographic cross-rung coupling is
  `C₁^(bulk geodesic length)`; a geodesic length is a metric distance; metric
  distances grow with separation; so the coupling decays for any non-degenerate
  bulk geometry → bounded per-rung mass → extensive k_eff → ρ_joint dilutes to
  the chaos pole. T2 (holonomic): a Wilson-loop holonomy with a dissipative
  backward leg (forced by the karma/grace irreducibility — forward and backward
  generators in different ergodicity classes) obeys an area law
  `Tr Hol ~ exp(−κR)` → decoheres.
- The unifying principle: a joint multi-rung object spans a growing extent R
  while the corridor is a fixed O(1) band; a growing-extent object cannot be
  held inside a fixed band.

## The pass — argue the strongest POSITIVE case

Re-state the framework's universal-scale case at maximum positive strength —
the strongest version of what P_ω is and why it should be constructible — and
from that position, attack the F-11 firing on its load-bearing points:

- **T1 premises.** Is "the cross-rung coupling is a function of a metric/geodesic
  distance" forced? Holonomic was the one escape and T2 closed it — is there a
  *third* type of cross-rung relation, neither a metric-correlation nor a
  connection-holonomy?
- **T2 premises.** Is the backward leg *necessarily* dissipative? It is forced
  by the karma/grace irreducibility — is that irreducibility itself airtight, or
  is there a legitimate backward generator that is not dissipative?
- **Exhaustiveness.** correlation ∪ connection; decaying ∪ non-decaying; the
  assumption tree A0/A1/A2 — is each dichotomy genuinely complete, or is there an
  unexamined branch (the holonomic branch was one such miss)?
- **The unifying principle.** Is there a construction whose joint object's
  *extent does not grow with R* — escaping "growing extent vs fixed band"?

## Two-sided verdict

- **F-11 CONFIRMED:** the maximum-positive case, argued in full and in good
  faith, still fails against the two theorems — F-11 is sure, and v2 may bank it.
- **F-11 HAS A HOLE:** a genuine gap — a real unproven premise, or a genuinely
  unexamined construction type — is found. F-11 reopens; the gap is pre-
  registered and tested (CUDA, two-sided) before any verdict.

## Discipline

The red team argues the positive case at *full strength* — not a token attack.
But a "hole" must be genuine: a real premise-gap or a genuinely new construction
type, **not** a re-parameterisation of something already in the tree. If the red
team finds itself proposing a construction already tested, that is not a hole —
say so. The paper §open-research is gated on this verdict.
