## Literature — the honest current state (verified citations)

The original excess has **moderated substantially** with sample size; the surviving anomaly is
contested (A_ISW between ~1 and ~5, significance ≤ 2.6σ where claimed, null in the largest
surveys):

- **Granett, Neyrinck & Szapudi 2008** (ApJ 683, L99; SDSS DR6 LRG, 50 supervoids + 50
  superclusters, ZOBOV, mean z ≈ 0.5): ΔT = 9.6 ± 2.2 μK on a compensated top-hat, **~4σ**,
  amplitude **~2–5× above the ΛCDM expectation** — the original "excess."
- **Granett, Kovács & Hawken 2015** (MNRAS 454, 2804; BOSS CMASS superstructures): template
  amplitude λ = 0.33 ± 0.17, GNS supervoids detected at only 2.4σ; **"unable to confirm the
  existence of anomalous ISW sources" — consistent with ΛCDM.**
- **Nadathur & Crittenden 2016** (BOSS DR12, potential-binned stacking, Big MD calibration):
  **A_ISW = 1.64 ± 0.53 — consistent with ΛCDM**, no anomalous excess.
- **Kovács et al. 2017** ("The part and the whole", MNRAS 475, 1777; arXiv:1701.08583) and
  **Kovács et al. 2019** (DES supervoids, 0.2 < z < 0.9): **A_ISW ≈ 5.2 ± 1.6, 3.3σ detection,
  ~2.6σ tension with ΛCDM** — the persistent excess claim, from specifically-selected supervoids.
- **Hang, Alam, Cai & Peacock 2021** (MNRAS 507, 510; DESI Legacy Survey, 0 < z < 0.8):
  **A_ISW = 0.68 ± 0.50 combined** (voids −0.10 ± 0.69, clusters 1.52 ± 0.72) — **fully
  consistent with ΛCDM; explicitly do NOT reproduce the several-times-ΛCDM excess.**

**Bottom line on the literature:** original ~2–5× excess at ~4σ (Granett 2008) → moderated to
consistency-with-ΛCDM in the largest re-measurements (Granett 2015, Nadathur & Crittenden 2016,
Hang 2021), with Kovács et al. still reporting A_ISW ≈ 5 at ≤ 2.6σ tension in a specific DES
supervoid selection. The excess is not robustly established today.
