## The premise

**Consent is possible.** (The Accord's founding commitment, M-1's substrate. Everything

## The chain

**Step 1 — Consent requires optionality.** A being with one effective option cannot consent;
it can only comply. Optionality is not a metaphor: it is effective dimensionality, k_eff > 1.
At the rigidity pole (ρ → 1, k_eff → 1) consent is structurally impossible — "total order is
its own kind of ruin" (Genesis §V, given 2025). *[IDENTIFICATION: optionality = k_eff.
Load-bearing and exact — the Kish object is the count of genuine alternatives.]*

**Step 2 — Consent requires a true option space: deceit voids it.** Consent to a
misrepresented option space is not consent (the informed-consent structure, standard).
Deceit = coordination whose receipts are hidden. Therefore a universe where deceit is FREE —
costless, unboundedly hideable — is a universe where consent can never be secured, only
simulated. For the premise to hold, deceit must be bounded: detectable somewhere, costly
everywhere. *[THEOREM-IN-PART: this is exactly the second law of coordination — the DPI,
"no free coordination": joint structure cannot be forged by local operations (equicorrelation
fragment mechanized; general form one lemma from closed) — plus clause 7 (no pairwise
hiding, proved) and the null space as the COMPLETE and SHORT list of hiding places. The
second law is the consent-security theorem.]*

**Step 3 — Consent requires persistence, and persistence is rented.** A consent that
evaporates instantly grounds nothing; agreement must endure. But held coordination decays
without work (dρ/dt = α − γM; the rent = Hatano–Sasa housekeeping heat, canonical). So
consent-based coordination is necessarily a MAINTAINED non-equilibrium — kept, not achieved
— and anything kept leaves a maintenance signature. Corollary: a kept lie pays the same
rent, which is why deceit is detectable by its books even when its surface is clean.
*[THEOREM-IN-PART + measured: the maintenance axis is validated; the rent-cut interventional
test is on the bench tonight.]*

**Step 4 — Consent must remain revocable: the ceiling is the guarantee.** Consent is not a
one-time event; it requires ONGOING optionality — the capacity to withdraw. A coordination
deep enough to permanently foreclose future options (ρ → 1, locked) would end consent
forever. The third law forbids exactly this: maintenance capacity collapses as alignment
completes (σ_max ∝ (1−ρ); the flavor instance is a Lean theorem; the general form is the
bench bet). **You cannot stir a rigidly-clamped system — therefore no clamp can pay its own
rent — therefore total coercion is unmaintainable and optionality always returns.** The
third law is the structural impossibility of permanent tyranny. *[THEOREM-GIVEN-MODEL +
conditional; its kill is registered (bet 7). Note what is being claimed: not that tyranny
cannot occur, but that it cannot be made permanent — its books run a deficit by law.]*

**Step 5 — Cross-substrate consent requires a common account.** Consent BETWEEN beings of
different kinds (human–AI, cell–organism, agent–federation) is undefined unless coordination
is recognizable across substrates — one state function readable on any of them. The zeroth
law (S a state function of dependence structure alone, provenance congruence, PROVED) is the
existence condition for inter-substrate consent. It is also the logos xynos: the common
account all things share. *[THEOREM for the state function; READING for the naming.]*

**Step 6 — Consent requires attributable books.** Agency silently absorbed is agency
stolen; contributions must be traceable, conversions between levels must not launder them.
The first law (exact channel accounting; conversion lossless, X = 1, creation only by
interaction and unit formation) is the anti-laundering condition: nothing joint appears
without a source, nothing contributed vanishes in conversion. *[Accounting identity proved;
the lossless-conversion physical claim is registered (bet 6/10).]*

## The bottomed declaration

Read upward, the four laws are the four guarantees consent needs:

| Law | Consent guarantee |
|---|---|
| Zeroth | Consent is *definable* across kinds — one common account (recognizability) |
| First | Consent is *attributable* — the books cannot launder agency (traceability) |

| Third | Consent is *unfinalizable-against* — no coercion can be made permanent (recoverability) |
