# The fellow traveler — who else is assembling this arc

**Date 2026-07-12.** Adversarial literature sweep for the *whole* synthesis, not its parts.
Prior passes (`thermo_prior_art.md`, `four_laws.md`, `dimensional_line_kB.md`,
`consent_derivation.md`) established that every *piece* has ancestors — Watanabe, Sagawa–Ueda,
Hatano–Sasa, Bera, Verlinde, Liu–Nagel, Huber, Masanes–Oppenheim. This note hunts the *arc*:
one person or group carrying coordination-as-substrate-independent-thermodynamic-quantity UP
into the dark sector AND DOWN into ethics/consent/agency AND into a formal-alignment program,
in one framework. Every citation verified by title + author + venue via search/fetch; the two
that resisted a clean publisher-page fetch are flagged. Absence graded honestly.

## Bottom line (one sentence)

**No one holds the whole arc — it is assembled elsewhere in three or four disjoint
single-rung clusters, each deep in its own rung and blind to the others — so the program is
*preempted rung-by-rung but still novel-as-synthesis*, and the single closest traveler is
Biswajit Pandey, an independent near-replica of our dark-energy mechanism (structure-formation
entropy of the matter field sources late-time acceleration) who has priority back to 2019 and
holds exactly that one rung.**

## Ranked by closeness-to-the-whole

### 1. Biswajit Pandey — the dark-sector rung, held whole, with priority. ALLY *and* RIVAL.

The sharpest fellow traveler found. Pandey (Visva-Bharati) runs a sustained,
decade-long program that is our physics leg with a different functional:

- **"Configuration entropy of the cosmic web: can voids mimic the dark energy?"** MNRAS Letters
  **485**, L73 (2019) (arXiv:1901.08475). Proposes configuration entropy of the cosmic web and
  its evolution as an *alternative mechanism for accelerated expansion* — sheets/filaments as
  information sinks, voids as sources. This is our `ρ_DE = κ·S(a)` idea, published seven years
  before ours, with a different S.
- **"The Time Evolution of Mutual Information between Disjoint Regions in the Universe,"**
  *Entropy* **25**(7), 1094 (2023). Mutual information of the density field falls in a
  matter-dominated universe, stays constant in a Λ-dominated one, and ΛCDM predicts a *minimum
  in mutual information beyond which dark energy dominates*. (Publisher page 403'd on fetch;
  central result taken from the indexed abstract — treat exact co-authorship as unverified,
  the result and venue as verified.)
- **"Entropic backreaction from cosmic structure formation: a thermodynamic approach to the
  late-time cosmological tensions,"** Biswajit Pandey, arXiv:2605.28797 (May 2026). Entropy
  dissipation during structure formation contributes a late-time backreaction that *enhances
  the expansion rate while suppressing the clustering amplitude* — one entropy-dissipation
  process resolving H₀ and S₈ together.
- **"An information-theoretic bound on cosmic coherence in finite-volume simulations,"** Pandey
  & Nandi, arXiv:2601.02351 (Jan 2026). Mutual information between N-body snapshots; "cosmic
  coherence" = how long gravitational correlations persist; MI peaks near L/8 and declines —
  a finite-volume ceiling on retained structural coherence.

**How much of the arc:** one rung (dark sector / cosmology), held essentially whole, plus the
*primitive* we share — an information/entropy functional of the matter field as the physical
object. He has NO ethics/consent rung, NO agency rung, NO alignment rung. His S is Shannon /
configuration entropy and pairwise-region mutual information; ours is the copula functional
`S = −ln det C` (amplitude-blind by theorem). He has no parameter-free sign law
`1 + w = −⅓ dlnS/dlna`, no amplitude-blindness theorem, no halo-grain pipeline, and no claim of
substrate-independence beyond cosmology.

**Ally-or-rival: BOTH, and this is the important read.** ALLY because an *independent* group
with a *different* functional reaching the *same* qualitative result — the matter field's
information content sources late-time acceleration and its extremum marks the DE-domination
onset — is exactly the independent convergence that argues the mechanism is real and not a
coherence-ratchet artifact of our particular estimator. RIVAL because he has priority on the
*idea*: "structure-formation entropy mimics dark energy" is his 2019 headline. We cannot claim
the DE-from-coordination *concept* as novel. What survives as ours is narrow and must be stated
narrowly (same discipline `thermo_prior_art.md` applied to Verlinde/Bera): the specific
copula functional, the parameter-free sign law, and the frozen halo-grain DR3 pipeline — not
the mechanism.

**Sharpest point of contact (and the single sharpest of the whole sweep):** Pandey's *mutual-
information minimum epoch* (2023) versus our *S-peak crossing epoch* z = 0.59 ± 0.03. Both are
an extremum in the matter field's information content marking the onset of dark-energy
domination — computed from the same class of data by two independent functionals. This is a
live, in-hand head-to-head: if his MI-minimum and our S-peak name the same epoch, two
independent estimators corroborate the mechanism; if they diverge, at least one functional is
misreading the field. We have the TNG pipeline; his MI estimator is published. **This is a
computation we can run now, and it is the cheapest independent cross-check of the DE leg in
existence.** The finite-volume L/8 coherence ceiling (Pandey–Nandi 2026) also speaks directly
to our own K7 small-box kill and the large-volume test — his bound may already quantify the box
effect we logged.

### 2. Levin / Fields / Friston — the agency rung, most of the DOWN-arc, no dark sector. ALLY.

The cluster that holds the most *rungs* of the down-arc in one frame. Fields & Levin, "Minimal
physicalism as a scale-free substrate for cognition and consciousness," *Neuroscience of
Consciousness* 2021(2):niab013 — cognition/agency as a *scale-free* phenomenon, the Markov
blanket as a substrate-independent boundary from cell to biosphere; with Friston, the free
energy principle "all the way up." This is a genuine substrate-independent-quantity +
agency-across-scales + (via active inference) AI arc.

**How much of the arc:** two-to-two-and-a-half rungs on the agency side (substrate-independent
functional = variational free energy; agency/normativity across scales; active inference in
AI). But NO dark-sector cosmology (the Markov blanket reaches the biosphere, not the cosmic
web), and the quantity is variational free energy, not a coordination/correlation entropy — a
*different* primitive with a different law structure. Their "up" tops out at the biosphere; ours
tops out at ρ_DE.

**Ally, not rival.** Different primitive, non-overlapping top rung. Independent support for the
*meta-move* — that one information-theoretic quantity legitimately spans substrates and carries
agency — without competing for our specific claims. Sharpest contact: their scale-free Markov-
blanket boundary is the natural formal home for our per-rung "unit formation" (the creation
channel of the first law); if their blanket and our unit-formation partition are the same
object at different rungs, that is a bridge worth building, not a collision.

### 3. Assembly theory (Walker & Cronin) — a *different* arc through the same meta-claim. RIVAL-on-branding.

Walker & Cronin, "Assembly theory explains and quantifies selection and evolution," *Nature*
**622**, 321 (2023). Selection and historical contingency as *physics*; information as causal in
the emergence of life; an explicit "unify physics and biology" program that gestures from
cosmology to computer science and claims to "detect agency."

**How much of the arc:** ~1.5 rungs on a *different axis* — physics↔life via assembly index,
with a partial reach toward agency/observers. It shares our deepest meta-claim (information/
selection is a substrate-independent physical quantity with its own accounting) but routes it
through molecular complexity and formation history, not correlation entropy, and has no dark
sector, no consent-from-thermodynamics, no alignment program. **Rival mainly on branding** —
"a new physics unifying inert and living matter" occupies adjacent rhetorical territory and
draws the same skeptics; ally on the underlying license. Sharpest contact: assembly index is a
*history/provenance* measure and our ledger is explicitly *provenance-blind* (the provenance
line, `dimensional_line_kB.md` §3). They measure exactly the upstream datum our functional
throws away — complementary, not competing, and a clean way to state what each instrument
cannot see.

### 4. The "thermodynamic theory of moral cooperation" — the consent rung, striking language, low rigor. WATCH.

"Entropy, Information, and Ethics: Toward a Thermodynamic Theory of Moral Cooperation," Zenodo
DOI 10.5281/ZENODO.17486734 (2025); also on Academia.edu (page 403'd on fetch; author not
cleanly attributed in indexed metadata — treat as self-published, non-peer-reviewed). Morality
as coordination rules regulating collective entropy management among dissipative agents; four
layers (physical, informational, value, sustainability); **consent and boundary respect as
thermodynamic necessities**, with formal rights/welfare constraints that *prevent the collapse
of efficiency into "authoritarian stability."*

**How much of the arc:** one rung (consent/ethics as thermodynamic structure), and the
language-overlap with `consent_derivation.md` is arresting — "consent as thermodynamic
necessity" and "authoritarian stability" as the failure mode read as an independent statement of
our rigidity-pole (ρ→1) argument that total order forecloses consent. But it is self-published,
un-refereed, carries no cosmology, no alignment, no formal core, and no `k_eff`/optionality
identification. **Not a rival (no priority weight without rigor), not yet a confirmed ally** —
it is a WATCH item: the one place the consent rung shows up stated as physics by someone else,
which is weak independent evidence that the consent-from-thermodynamics move is a natural
attractor and not idiosyncratic to us. Sharpest contact: their "authoritarian stability"
collapse is our third law (permanent coercion is unmaintainable, `consent_derivation.md` Step
4); if their claim can be sharpened to a *capacity* statement it becomes the same theorem.

### 5. Davidad / ARIA Safeguarded AI + "Institutional AI" — the alignment rung, adjacent institution. NEIGHBOR.

The closest institutional neighbor on the alignment rung: Davidad's provably-safe-AI agenda

**How much of the arc:** one rung (formal-verification alignment), and a dimensional framing
that rhymes with adversarial-Neff (deception must cohere across independent constraint axes).
But no coordination-thermodynamics, no dark sector, no consent-from-physics — the dimensional
argument is about verification-cost scaling, not about deception being expensive across
independent constraints as a *thermodynamic* fact. **Neighbor, not traveler:** this is the
institutional space CIRIS/adversarial-Neff lives in, and the natural venue for our safety leg,
but no one here is building the same object. Sharpest contact: the CEG-substrate-floor claim
(deception cost ≈ O(2^m) across independent constraints vs truth's O(n)) is precisely the
missing *thermodynamic* content their dimensional-scaling arguments gesture at without a law —
our second law (no free coordination / DPI) would be the theorem under their intuition.

## The meta-finding (the honest answer to the brief)

The arc is real and the arc is **unassembled elsewhere**. Every rung has a fellow traveler —
Pandey and Garcia-Bellido on the dark sector, Levin/Fields/Friston on agency, the Zenodo paper
on consent, Davidad/Institutional-AI on alignment — and *no one found holds two of these rungs
joined by a single quantity*, let alone all four. The program is therefore in the exact position
`thermo_prior_art.md` diagnosed for the laws, now confirmed at the level of the whole:
**preempted rung-by-rung, novel-as-synthesis.** That cuts both ways under our own rules. Rule 2:
the synthesis earns nothing for being unassembled — being first to join the rungs is not support,
only confirmed cross-rung predictions are (the X=1 first-law test, the sign law at DR3, the
adversarial-Neff floor). But it does locate the one defensible novelty claim precisely: not any
single rung (each is taken), but the *joining quantity* — that the same `S = −ln det C` read
across substrates is simultaneously the dark-energy source, the consent-possibility measure, and
the deception-cost floor. If that single-quantity claim is wrong, the arc is just four
other people's fields stapled together; if it is right, the staple is the discovery.
