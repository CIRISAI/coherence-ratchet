## The error

**S is blind to amplitude. It is a functional of the copula, not the marginals.**

Shock compression is a *density* (amplitude) change. S cannot see it. What a shock does to
the *correlation* structure is the opposite of what the note assumed: it **thermalizes**.

## What the shock actually does to coordination

- Merger shocks **sharply increase the gas entropy** (two distinct episodes, per merger
  simulations; the ICM is heated to ~10⁸ K, X-ray bright). Thermalization drives the velocity
  distribution toward Maxwellian — i.e. toward **independence** among fluid elements.
- Maximum thermodynamic entropy at fixed energy = minimum mutual information. So the shocked
  plasma is the component with **low S**, not high S.
- The **collisionless** components (galaxies, and CDM if it exists) pass through. Liouville
  preserves their fine-grained phase-space density; the galaxies retain a coherent bulk
  velocity (~4500 km/s). Coherent bulk motion is *perfectly correlated* motion. They are the
  component with **high S**.

**At the collisionless, kinematically coherent component.** The lensing mass in 1E 0657-56 is
coincident with the collisionless galaxies and *ahead of* the collisional gas, at ~8σ.

**The ledger points at the lensing mass.** The reading survives its most famous killer.
